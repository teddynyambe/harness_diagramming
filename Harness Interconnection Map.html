How it works: edit anything below and the diagram above redraws instantly. Each harness box shows its connectors as ports on its boundary; the small badge on a port is the circuit count (hover a port to list them). Set a connector's “Connects to” dropdown to draw the mating line. Add circuits inside any connector with “+ circuit”. Add a connector photo by clicking its image box, or drop <key>.jpg into harness_connectors/ (key shown in the empty box).
